Medical Self Checkin Kiosk
What did I build?

I developed and deployed a self checkin kiosk for a medical clinic to support our virtual reception team at Advance Healthcare. This cloud-hosted app is used on iPads at several of our clinics, and the app interacts seamlessly with our practice management software to mark these appointments as "arrived".

The interface had to be robust and intuitive enough to enable patients of all ages and backgrounds to use it effectively.

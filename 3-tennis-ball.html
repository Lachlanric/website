Tennis Ball Collecting Mobile Robot
What did we build?

We set out to design and construct an autonomous mobile robot which collects tennis balls inside a tennis court quadrant and deposits them in a cardboard box. The project was subject to time, cost and materials constraints, prompting us to make several difficult tradeoffs to ensure reliable operation during the final demonstration for the project unit.
